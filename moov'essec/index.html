Who we are ?

Moov'Essec is an internal ESSEC platform. The Moov’Essec application is designed on the basis of the allocation of credits called “Mooves”. These are also awarded based on the weekly number of steps taken by each individual in the ESSEC community. In fact, depending on the different partnerships obtained, prizes, considered as rewards, could be collected by participants once they reach 20,000 steps. Also, the higher the number of steps taken, the greater the rewards could be.
Moov'ESSEC is avalaible on Cergy, Rabat and Singapour campuses.
